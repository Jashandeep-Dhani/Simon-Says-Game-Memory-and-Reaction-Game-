Simon Says Game

Simon Says is a fun and simple game of listening and quick reactions.
Here's how to play: One person is chosen to be "Simon" and gives instructions to the other players.
If the instruction starts with "Simon says", players must follow it.
Example: “Simon says touch your nose” - everyone touches their nose.
If the instruction doesn't start with “Simon says,” players must not follow it.
Example: “Jump!” - anyone who jumps is out.
Players who follow a command incorrectly or miss a correct one are eliminated.
The last player remaining is the winner!
It's a great game to test attention and listening skills!

🕹️ How to Play

Press any key to start the game.
Watch the flashing button and remember the color sequence.
Click the buttons in the same order as shown.
Advance to the next level by repeating the correct sequence.
Make a mistake? It's Game Over - your score is the level you reached.
